Our Story

More Than a Motel.
A Family Promise.

“We didn't just build a place to stay — we built a place to belong. Every guest who walks through our doors becomes part of the RoseLoe family.”

Nestled between the ancient ridges of the Allegheny Highlands, RoseLoe Motel was born from a simple belief: that the best hospitality comes from the heart. While the big hotel chains offer uniformity, we offer something rarer — genuine warmth, personal attention, and the kind of care that only a family-run establishment can provide.

From freshly prepared rooms each morning to kitchen-equipped suites that let you cook like you're at home, every detail is designed with your comfort in mind. The mountains outside your window aren't just a view — they're an invitation to explore one of Virginia's most beautiful and unspoiled regions.
