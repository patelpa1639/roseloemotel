Welcome

Tucked away in the scenic mountains of Virginia, RoseLoe Motel is your perfect home base for adventures in Hot Springs and Warm Springs. Whether you're here for the legendary thermal springs, world-class golf, or simply the peace of the Allegheny Highlands, we offer comfortable rooms, genuine hospitality, and unbeatable value.

As a family-friendly motel, we provide the ideal start and end point for excursions into the countryside — perfect for hikers, cyclists, and those seeking the beauty of rural Virginia.

Local Attractions

Discover Bath County,
Virginia

From natural thermal springs to championship golf, our corner of Virginia offers unforgettable experiences just minutes from your door.

Jefferson Pools

Historic natural warm spring pools dating back centuries, once visited by Thomas Jefferson himself.

Cascades Golf Course

World-renowned championship course at The Homestead, set against stunning mountain backdrops.

Hidden Valley Trail

Scenic hiking trails through pristine Virginia wilderness with breathtaking mountain vistas.

Garth Newel Music Center

Intimate chamber music performances in a beautiful countryside setting year-round.

Fishing & Hunting

Bath County's rivers and forests offer some of the finest fishing and hunting in the state.

Cycling & More

Miles of scenic routes for cyclists of all levels through the beautiful Allegheny Highlands.
